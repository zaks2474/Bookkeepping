AGENT-FORENSIC-001 Phase 1 — Step 1.9: Rate Limiting
=====================================================
Date: 2026-02-02

CONFIGURATION (from config.py and rate_limiter.py):
- Backend: slowapi with memory:// storage (in-process, not shared across workers)
- Strategy: fixed-window
- Client identification: IP + auth token hash

Endpoint Rate Limits:
  /                         — 10 per minute
  /health                   — 20 per minute
  /api/v1/auth/register     — 10 per hour
  /api/v1/auth/login        — 20 per minute
  /api/v1/chatbot/chat      — 30 per minute
  /api/v1/chatbot/chat/stream — 20 per minute
  /api/v1/chatbot/messages  — 50 per minute
  /api/v1/agent/invoke      — 50 per minute
  /api/v1/agent/invoke/stream — 30 per minute
  /api/v1/agent/approvals (list) — 60 per minute
  /api/v1/agent/approvals/{id} — 60 per minute

  /api/v1/agent/approvals/{id}:reject — 30 per minute
  /api/v1/agent/threads/{id}/state — 60 per minute
  Default: 100 per minute

LIVE TEST:

  Request 21-25: returned 429 (rate limit exceeded)
