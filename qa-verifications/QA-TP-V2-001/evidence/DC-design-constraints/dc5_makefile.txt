=== DC-5: Makefile Integration ===
--- DC-5.1: Targets follow make conventions ---
tripass-init: ## Initialize TriPass pipeline (templates + CLI discovery)
tripass-run: ## Run TriPass pipeline (usage: make tripass-run MISSION=<file> MODE=forensic)
tripass-status: ## Show TriPass pipeline status (usage: make tripass-status RUN_ID=<id>)
tripass-gates: ## Re-run gates for a TriPass run (usage: make tripass-gates RUN_ID=<id>)

--- DC-5.2: settings.json allows make * patterns ---
      "Bash(make sync-*)",
      "Bash(make validate-*)",
      "Bash(make infra-*)",
      "Bash(make update-*)"
